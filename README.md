## 👋 Hey there! Let me introduce myself

I'm **Satyam Shivam**, a B.Tech student diving deep into the fascinating world of **Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning** at United Institute of Technology. What gets me excited? Building systems that don't just work—but actually *understand* and *learn*.

Think of me as someone who's obsessed with making AI systems smarter and more reliable. I spend most of my time crafting **LLM-powered backend systems** and **intelligent automation tools** that solve real-world problems. There's something magical about watching a machine comprehend context and deliver spot-on answers!

### What I'm up to these days:

- 🚀 **Currently building:** Multi-agent AI systems and production-ready RAG pipelines that actually solve real-world problems
- 📚 **Learning journey:** Deep-diving into GraphRAG, AI orchestration, and making autonomous agents more reliable
- 🤝 **Open to collaborate:** If you're working on AI/ML projects, NLP applications, or anything involving intelligent systems—let's talk!
- 💡 **Love discussing:** Multi-agent architectures, FastAPI magic, the art of prompt engineering, vector databases, and everything RAG
- 🎯 **My philosophy:** AI is only as good as the context it understands. That's why I'm passionate about building systems that are grounded, observable, and auditable
- ☕ **Beyond code:** When I'm not training models or debugging pipelines, you'll find me exploring new AI research papers or experimenting with the latest agentic frameworks

*Jun 2025 - Jul 2025*

This is where theory met reality! I got to build production-grade AI chatbots that people actually use. Here's what I accomplished:

- 🤖 **Built from scratch:** Document-based AI chatbots using cutting-edge LLM and NLP pipelines
- 🔄 **Complete RAG implementation:** Designed and deployed end-to-end workflows—from PDF ingestion to embeddings, vector search, and finally, intelligent LLM responses
- 🎯 **Made AI reliable:** Significantly improved answer accuracy by grounding responses in retrieved, verified context (no more hallucinations!)
